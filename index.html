About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website!

Here you will find my projects as a web design graduate student for University of Florida and also my previous work as an assistant director.

After working in the tourism industry for some years, I took a leap of faith and decided to pursue a new career. Don’t get me wrong, I love traveling. But I decided it was time to turn traveling into a hobby and my design passion into a career. I chose to go back to school and I was accepted in the graduate program for Web Design at University of Florida for fall 2020.

Design is something I’ve been interested all my life, an interest that grew into passion once I started my career. Throughout my professional life I had the opportunity to do product development and work closely with designers, putting my passion into practice on the sidelines by doing newsletters, flyers and brochures.

My goal is to master all design technologies and become a well-rounded web designer. At the moment I’m learning to build beautiful, accessible and SEO friendly websites, built according to the latest web standards. I’m looking forward to put my knowledge into practice.

Feel free to have a look at my portfolio and don’t hesitate to contact me if you think I can be of service to you. Thanks for stopping by!
